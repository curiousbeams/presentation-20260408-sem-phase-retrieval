Diffraction intensities are modeled using a physical forward model.
The sample phase is then updated to minimize the difference between the model and the measured intensities.

:::{math}
:label: eq_single_slice_model
\begin{align*}
\psi_{\mathrm{exit}}^{(j)}(\bm{r}) &= \mathcal{P(r)} \times \mathcal{O}(\bm{r}-\bm{R}_j) \\
I^{(j)}(\bm{k}) &= \left|\mathcal{F}_{\bm{r} \rightarrow \bm{k}} \left\{\psi_{\mathrm{exit}}^{(j)}(\bm{r})\right\}\right|^2
\end{align*}
:::
