Direct ptychography can be formulated as a loop over $\bm{k}$.
Since $\Gamma(\bm{q,k})$ has compact support, this loop is restricted to the BF disk $\bm{k}_{\mathrm{BF}}$.
The reconstruction thus accumulates deconvolved virtual BF images [@10.48550/arXiv.2507.18610].
