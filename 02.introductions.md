The [Curious Beams Lab](https://cbl.curve.space/) is a budding research group at the [TU Delft Imaging Physics department](https://www.tudelft.nl/tnw/over-faculteit/afdelingen/imphys/).
Our goal is to make high-resolution electron microscopy robust and accessible by combining advanced simulation, analysis, and instrumentation.
