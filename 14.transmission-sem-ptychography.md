::::{tab-item} Challenges vs STEM Ptychography

Electron Optics Differences:
- Schottky sources offer reduced coherence compared to Cold FEGs.
- Unlike high-end S/TEMs, standard SEMs lack accessible aberration correction.
- The lack of a projector lens results in significantly smaller camera lengths.

Transfer of Information Differences:
- Small convergence angles prevent the overlap of diffracted disks.
- Standard weak-phase object methods fail under these specific conditions.
- Reconstructions must instead rely on "dark" electrons and shape factors.
