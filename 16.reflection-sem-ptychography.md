::::{tab-item} Coherent Backscattered Electrons

Diffractive imaging in reflection offers a plausible path toward atomic-scale analysis for bulk samples.
By utilizing accurate forward models and specialized optics to isolate elastic backscattered electrons, this approach could unlock sub-angstrom resolution.

::::{tab-item} Miniaturized Reflection Optics

Compact MEMS separators will be fabricated to deflect backscattered electrons by over 500 mrad at low energies. 
These will utilize stacked E–B-E geometries to precisely isolate and filter elastically scattered electrons [@https://doi.org/10.1063/5.0008089].
