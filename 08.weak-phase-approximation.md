Under the weak phase-object approximation (WPOA)
:::{math}
:label: eq_wpoa
\psi_{\mathrm{exit}}(\bm{r}) \approx 1 + \mathrm{i} \sigma V_p(\bm{r}) + \mathcal{O}(\sigma^2 V_p^2(\bm{r}))
:::

The Fourier-transformed diffraction intensity is expressed as [@https://doi.org/cm9m8z]
:::{math}
:label: eq_forward_model
\begin{align*}
G(\bm{q,k}) &= |\psi(\bm{k})|^2 \delta(
\bm{q}) + \Gamma(\bm{q,k}) \phi(\bm{q}) \\
\Gamma(\bm{q,k}) &= \psi^*(\bm{k})\psi(\bm{k-q}) - \psi(\bm{k}) \psi^*(\bm{k+q}) \\
\psi(\bm{k}) &= A(\bm{k}) \mathrm{e}^{-\mathrm{i} \chi(\bm{k})}
\end{align*}
:::
